Embed the WebReserv booking/availability calendar 2.0 seamlessly in any Wordpress PAGE or POST.

== Description ==

WebReserv just developed a new version of its booking calendar. This plugin lets you embed the new booking calendar 2.0 directly in any PAGE or POST.

 
WebReserv is a cloud-based booking system. It's easy to set up and use. With WebReserv, you can take bookings 24/7 and the system can be accessed from your office, your home or on the road. All you need is an Internet connection. WebReserv works for small and medium businesses of almost any industry. It doesn't matter if your business is a bed and breakfast, a Segway tour or a boat rental company. If it is possible to book or reserve your products/services, then WebReserv can help you making the reservation process easier.

For a full list of supported industries see [Other Notes](http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/webreserv-booking-calender-plugin/other_notes/)

**WebReserv Booking System Features**

* Works great with nearly any industry
* Supports several calendar formats: Hourly, daily, nightly, fixed/flexible schedule
* Automatic emails notifications to the business when bookings are made
* Automatic and configurable pending and confirmed emails to your customers
* Real time inventory management - when something is booked, it is marked automatically as unavailable
* Ability to take reservations for walk-ins/phone/email inquiries using the back office
* Convenient all-in-one online reservation management system
* Multiple Reports that can be printed or downloaded (availability, revenue, reservation, in-out, etc.)
* Support for  seasonal products and rates
* Set up discount codes
* Sell gift cards
* Offer additional services or add ons using custom fields
* Booking component can be embedded directly on a webpage
* Handles several payment methods
	* Cash/Check
	* Credit Card Capture
	* Processing Gateways
	* PayPal
	* RealexPayments

For a complete listing of features see [Complete List of WebReserv Features](http://www.webreserv.com/features.do)

== Installation ==

Installing the WebReserv Embedded Booking Calendar plugin is easy.

1.	Install the plugin

2.	Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

3.	Go to **Settings** – **WebReserv 2.0** and follow the steps. All products, times, rates, etc. are configured in the WebReserv back office. [Click here](http://www.webreserv.eu/help) to access our online help.



== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I test the plugin without creating a WebReserv Account? =

Yes, the default installation is associated with a demo business so you can paste a demo calendar on a POST or PAGE right away.

= What types of bookings does the Widget Support? =

It supports any type of booking the WebReserv system can handle. The WebReserv booking and reservation system can handle, minute, hourly, daily, weekly, monthly and fixed/flexible schedule. It works for nearly any industry such as lodging, property management, rentals, campgrounds, RVs, tours, etc. In other words, anything that is bookable can be booked through WebReserv. For a full list of supported industries see [Supported Industries](http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/webreserv-booking-calender-plugin/other_notes/) .

= Do I need to have a server installed or install new software? =

No, our services are completely hosted and managed by us. All you need is a browser and an Internet connection.

= How long does it take to setup my website =

Setting up your account is very easy and can usually be accomplished in just a few hours.

= Where can I find information and help for the setup? =

We have an extensive online help and we are constantly updating it to provide you the best information. Check our online help [here](http://www.webreserv.com/help) 

= I also take reservations in my office, how do I ensure that I don't get overbooked? =

WebReserv handles your inventory; the booking calendar is always synchronized with your administration system, so there is no risk of double-bookings.

= I need one day notice when reservations are made on the Internet reservation system, is that possible? =

Yes, you can configure a minimum/maximum time in advance that a reservation will be accepted.

= I would like to get a deposit for some of my products, is that possible? =

Yes, you can configure a product to require a deposit before a reservation is made. Also it is possible to automate your payments with PayPal or credit card.

= Do I need to process the credit card information when deposits are made? =

You can make the credit card deposit transactions yourself or you can have it done automatically when the reservation is made.

= Can I approve a reservation before it's confirmed? =

Yes, you can configure the system to make reservations as pending, thereby allowing you to manually confirm or reject the reservation.

= How many products can I have on the reservation website? =

It depends on the package:
-	[Check our packages](http://www.webreserv.eu/products_webreserv.do) if your business is based in Europe.
-	[Check our packages](http://www.webreserv.com/features.do) if your business is based in US, Asia or 
Australia.


= Do you support Spanish or other languages? =

Yes, multiple languages and currencies are supported. Currently the booking component runs in English, Spanish, German, French, Danish, Swedish, Portuguese, Norwegian, Dutch and Italian.

= Is my customers information secure? =

Our systems are PCI-DSS compliant and uses leading-edge 128-encryption for data transmission and storage. The WebReserv booking system is securely hosted in a leading-edge datacenter and continuously being backed up. Your data is safe with us.

= If there is an issue or I have questions, do you provide support? =

Our highest priority is to provide you with a safe, secure and reliable booking system and we are here when you need us. Your WebReserv account includes free phone support, email support and access to all online resources.
